SERVICES

What we run on the floor.

Every service below runs through the same inspection process — so a one-off prototype gets the same scrutiny as unit 4,000 of a production order.

01 / MILLING

CNC Milling

3-axis through 5-axis simultaneous milling for aluminum, steel, stainless, titanium, and engineering plastics. Suited to brackets, housings, manifolds, and complex geometry that needs multiple faces machined without re-fixturing.

Tolerance to ±0.005mm · Envelope to 800×500×500mm
02 / TURNING

CNC Turning

Live-tool lathes for shafts, bushings, spindles, and other rotationally symmetric parts, with milling operations done in the same setup to hold concentricity.

Ø3mm–200mm · Bar-fed or chucked
03 / GRINDING

Precision Grinding

Surface and cylindrical grinding for mating faces, seal diameters, and finishes tighter than milling or turning can hold on their own.

Ra 0.2 achievable · Flatness to 0.005mm
04 / ASSEMBLY

Assembly & Sub-assembly

Mechanical assembly of machined components, fasteners, bearings, and off-the-shelf hardware into finished or semi-finished units, built to a documented work instruction.

Torque-verified · Work instructions on file
05 / INSPECTION

Quality Inspection

CMM, pin gauges, micrometers, and surface finish testing against your drawing, with first-article inspection reports available for every new part number.

FAI + CofC on request
06 / FINISHING

Finishing & Coating

Anodizing, passivation, bead blasting, and painting arranged through vetted finishing partners, tracked under the same job traveler as the machining.

Type II & III anodize · Chem film
